A great way

to learn Spanish

Ceica Peru is a Spanish language school located in Arequipa, Peru.

We provide individual lessons for all levels of Spanish speakers and group lessons for new learners in a relaxed setting, in our beautiful garden or in the classroom. We have special courses for travellers, volunteers and missionaries. We also offer a variety of accommodation options, including living with local families, staying in the school building, and staying in a private furnished apartment.

What we offer

Courses

We have a variety of courses for all levels of Spanish, as well as special courses in Peruvian history, Peruvian literature and Quechua.

Read more >

Special programs

We also offer special courses in Spanish for travelling, and programms combining Spanish learning with speaking practice around the city of Arequipa.

Read more >

Volunteering

Ceica Peru Spanish School works together with several social projects in Arequipa to offer volunteer programs that allow you to practice Spanish and at the same time contribute to the social development of the children and adults of these communities.
