{% trans 'How it works' %}

  1. Go our enrollment page to begin your application. You'll be asked fill out your basic details and select the type of program you'd like to do, choosing the length of your stay and whether or not you'd like Spanish classes. You can then select your three most preferred volunteer projects. Please note that places are subject to availability.
  2. Once we have received the application form, we will ask you to send the documents which are requested by the relevant social organization. This could include a résumé in Spanish, a letter of Introduction about yourself in Spanish, or possibly a copy of your university degree or your certificate of studies.
  3. After a couple of days, we will send you a response telling you which organization has accepted you. You'll then be invited to choose your accommodation and complete your enrollment.
  4. With everything confirmed, we will be in touch with you by e-mail you to coordinate further details and plan your arrival.
