Program requirements

  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • You should obey the personnel in charge of your social project.
  • It's important to have an open mindset to understand the restrictions that our traditional conservative culture imposes, for example when you are working wear appropriate clothes (no miniskirts, shorts, low or skimpy tops etc); avoid romantic relationships with the personnel or youths in care.
  • Behave ethically in the volunteer work place and be willing to comply with the norms established by the institution.
